Web Animation

For this subject we had to choose a poster and made it digital and interactive. So we used HTML/CSS/JS to make it a website and to design it just like the real poster. I chose one that had many arrows and stars. We had to work with SVG's to I first designed alle the elements in Adobe Illustrator and exported them as an SVG. I've never worked with SVG's before this subject so I had spent a lot of time figuring that out. But it worked in the end.

After I made my poster online, I needed to animate it to make it interactive. So all the arrows are clickable and if you click on one of them, it will rotate and the information text will be visible. All those little stars are rotating infinitely and change to the color pink if you click on them. They also spin a little faster after clicking.

The interface had to be responsible, so I've worked with breakpoints to define my screen types. I designed my protoype for desktop, Ipad and Iphone.
Also, if you hover on one of those arrows, the arrow becomes pink. I also designed some key interactions, for example, if you press 1, the backround color will change, and if you press 6, something else will happen. I made a lot of key interactions to make it more fun.

Project Design 3

For the last project, we had to choose an online article about something you like, and we had to turn that into an online interactive design. I chose an article from The Correspondent, written by Vera Mulder. The article was about someone Vera Mulder met in Surinam, named Kenny. I designed a website around Vera Mulder and made it an online platform with all her articles about someone she has met in her life.

It starts with a big map of the world, and little dots on places that she went to. You can click on those dots to see what kind of people she met there and after that you can click on those peoples to read the artcile Vera made about them. I designed the article about Kenny, and made it interactive. I chose to design the article in a suiting context, the place she describes in her article. Down below is a link to my prototype.
